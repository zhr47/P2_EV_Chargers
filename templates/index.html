The Future Is Electric

A future where you will be powering your car at an alternative fuel station is closer than you might think. On Sept 23, 2020, Governor Gavin Newsom of California signed an executive order, phasing out the sale of new gasoline cars by 2035. The State of New Jersey is considering a similar ban, and by association, so is the state of New York, as it will not be outdone by the likes of New Jersey. EV charging stations are slowly but surely popping up everywhere. In fact, you might be surprised how many are already functional.
On September 26th, 2019, a gas station in Maryland became the first EV charging station in the US to convert from selling oil.
